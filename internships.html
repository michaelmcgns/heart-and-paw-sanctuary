All opportunities
The Program

More than a placement — a foundation

Heart & Paw internships are structured, supervised, and genuinely formative. Whether you are studying veterinary science, animal behaviour, communications, or nonprofit management — you will leave with real skills, real references, and real clarity about what you want to do next.

We work with interns as full members of the team, not as assistants. You will be trusted with responsibility from day one.

Internship tracks

Animal Science
Veterinary & Animal Behaviour
On-site 3–6 months

Work alongside our resident vet and animal behaviour team. Ideal for students of veterinary nursing, animal science, or zoology who want hands-on clinical and behavioural experience with rescue dogs.

You will gain
  • Hands-on clinical support skills
  • Trauma and behavioural rehabilitation techniques
  • Positive reinforcement training methodology
  • Formal reference from our lead veterinarian

Communications
Marketing & Fundraising
Remote or on-site Stipend available

Own our social channels, write adoption profiles, build email campaigns, and help drive our donor acquisition. Ideal for marketing, journalism, or communications students who want a real portfolio piece with real impact.

You will gain
  • Full ownership of social media strategy

  • Storytelling and copywriting portfolio
  • Formal reference from our Communications Manager

Nonprofit Management
Operations & Impact
Hybrid Stipend available

Work across adoption coordination, donor relations, and operational planning. Ideal for students of nonprofit management, social enterprise, or business who want to understand how a values-driven organisation runs day to day.

You will gain
  • End-to-end adoption and fostering pipeline experience
  • Donor management and CRM skills
  • EU cross-border logistics and compliance knowledge
  • Formal reference from our Director, Daniella de Raaf
