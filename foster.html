Foster Program

Open your home, open a heart

Fostering is the single most powerful thing you can do for a dog who isn't ready for adoption yet — and it will change you too.

The Journey

How fostering works

From first enquiry to the day your foster dog finds their forever home — here is every step.

1

Apply online

Complete the form below. We review all applications within 5 working days.

2

Home assessment

A friendly video call with our adoption team to understand your home, lifestyle, and experience.

3

Meet your match

We carefully pair you with a dog whose needs and energy fit your life perfectly.

4

Welcome home

Your foster dog arrives with full medical records, food, and everything they need.

5

We support you

Our team is available 7 days a week throughout your fostering journey — no question too small.

What We Provide

Heart & Paw supports every foster carer

  • All food and supplies for the duration of fostering
  • Full veterinary coverage — we pay every bill
  • 24/7 behavioural support from our expert team
  • Training guidance tailored to your foster dog
  • Emergency backup — if life intervenes, we step in
  • A community of 150+ active foster carers across Europe
What We Ask of You

What makes a great foster carer?

  • A stable, dog-safe living environment (house or garden preferred)
  • Availability to be home for most of the day (at least initially)
  • Patience — some dogs take days to open up, others take months
  • Willingness to communicate openly with our team
  • Agreement to our fostering guidelines (provided on approval)
  • Prior dog experience is helpful but not required
