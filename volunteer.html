Your Experience

What to expect as a volunteer

We take care of our volunteers as carefully as we take care of our dogs.

1
Full orientation on arrival

Every volunteer receives a thorough introduction to the sanctuary, our dogs, and our approach before starting.

2
Staff supervision throughout

You are never left alone with a situation you're not equipped for. Our team is always close by.

3
Accommodation near the sanctuary

For stays of 5+ days we can connect you with local accommodation at reduced rates.

4
A reference letter on request

All volunteers who complete a minimum 5-day stay can request a formal reference from Daniella de Raaf.
