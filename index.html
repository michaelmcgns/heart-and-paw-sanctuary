The Sanctuary

A living garden of second chances

The Heart and Paw Sanctuary is a non-profit organization built to rescue any and all dogs in need. We are a no-kill forever home sanctuary located in the countryside region of Italy, and stem our rescues throughout the United States. We value health and happiness for all of our dogs, and ensure the best for them every day. All of the dogs at our center are up for adoption and we strive to ensure the safest and best fit homes for each and every one of them.

About Our Team

When developing our dream at Heart and Paws we ensured to build a family that is passionate about our mission and our goals. Every team member strives to support and love our dogs, our team, and our company.

European adoption network

Life at the Sanctuary

Where healing grows as wild as the land

We believe that dogs don't just need food and safety — they need soil underfoot, sun on their backs, and the freedom to simply be.

"We don't run a shelter. We tend a living garden, and every dog is a rare bloom waiting for the right gardener."

— Daniella de Raaf, Founder
I
Arrive, rest, breathe

Every dog enters a quiet quarantine garden — no crowds, no noise — and is never rushed.

II
Heal with the land

Behavioural therapy is conducted outdoors. The land itself is part of the treatment.

III
Join the family

Dogs integrate into small groups — their own pack, their own patch of paradise.

IV
Find their garden

We match each dog to an adopter whose home mirrors what they thrive in — for life.
