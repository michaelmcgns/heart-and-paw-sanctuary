Open Positions

Current openings

We offer both remote and on-site opportunities. On-site roles are based at our sanctuary near Siena; remote roles can be carried out from anywhere in Europe. Relocation support available for the right candidate.

Veterinary
Veterinary Nurse
Full-time On-site · Siena €28,000–€34,000

We are looking for a qualified veterinary nurse to join our on-site medical team. You will assist Dr. Conti with daily health checks, post-operative care, vaccination programmes, and the ongoing medical management of our 40–60 resident dogs.

Requirements
  • Qualified veterinary nurse (RVN or European equivalent)
  • Minimum 2 years clinical experience
  • Experience with dogs essential; rescue/shelter experience preferred
  • Italian or English essential; both a strong advantage
  • Genuine passion for animal welfare

Animal Care
Dog Handler & Enrichment Coordinator
Full-time On-site · Siena €22,000–€27,000

A hands-on role working directly with our resident dogs each day — leading walks, designing enrichment activities, supporting behavioural rehabilitation, and building the trust and confidence that every dog needs before adoption.

Requirements
  • Strong practical experience handling dogs of all sizes and temperaments
  • Understanding of positive reinforcement training methods
  • Physical fitness — the role involves outdoor work in all weather
  • Calm, patient temperament and excellent observational skills
  • Italian or English; both preferred

Communications
Social Media & Fundraising Manager
Full-time or Part-time Remote-friendly €24,000–€30,000

We have 1,200 success stories to tell and a community that genuinely cares. We need someone with warmth, creativity, and a head for fundraising to help us grow our donor base, tell our dogs' stories, and connect with adopters across Europe.

Requirements
  • Proven experience managing social media for a cause-driven or lifestyle brand
  • Strong copywriting in English (additional languages a huge plus)
  • Experience with donation platforms, email campaigns, or grant writing
  • Photography or video editing skills a strong advantage
  • Deep genuine alignment with animal welfare and our mission

Adoptions
Adoption & Foster Coordinator
Full-time Hybrid · Siena / Remote €24,000–€29,000

The bridge between our dogs and their new families. You will manage the full adoption and fostering pipeline — from first enquiry to home assessment, matching, transport coordination, and lifelong post-adoption support across 14 countries.

Requirements
  • Experience in animal adoption, social work, or community coordination
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • English essential; German, Dutch, or French a significant advantage
  • Organised, empathetic, and calm under emotional pressure
  • Familiarity with EU pet transport regulations preferred
